The Problem

As @jadi mentions : We have a Pyramid System where you insert people into system as your own subgroup and get paid a share of what your subgroup earns! As your subgroup will in turn insert others!

The Objective is to calculate the number of people who get paid and the number of months when the whole population will be members of the system!
